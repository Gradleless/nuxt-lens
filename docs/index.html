NuxtLens

Privacy Policy

Data collection

NuxtLens does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Page data

The extension reads __NUXT_DATA__ from the active tab only when you open the popup. This data is processed locally in your browser and never leaves your device.

Local storage

The snapshot feature saves store data in browser.storage.local on your device only. This data is never sent anywhere.

Permissions

  • activeTab / tabs — read page data from the current tab
  • storage — save snapshots locally on your device
  • scripting — inject the content script to detect Nuxt data
